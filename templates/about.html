About Our School

Shaping Futures Since 1993 Founded on 2nd July 1993 by Ramdas Bhosale Gurugi, our school has been a beacon of education, excellence, and values for over three decades. With a vision to provide quality education, we have nurtured generations of students, empowering them with knowledge, skills, and character. At our school, we believe in fostering an environment where learning goes beyond textbooks. Our commitment to academic excellence, cultural enrichment, and sports ensures that students grow into confident, responsible, and well-rounded individuals.

Proud Achievement in NMMS Scholarship Examination

We are thrilled to share that several of our bright and dedicated students have successfully qualified for the National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS) Examination, earning prestigious scholarships awarded by the Government of India.

About Our Founder

More than three decades ago, a visionary educator, Ramdas Bhosale Guruji, laid the foundation of Chhatrapati Shivaji Vidyamandir, Dhayati. With a deep commitment to spreading the light of education in rural areas, he dedicated his life to building a school that would empower generations through knowledge and values. Guruji was not only an inspiring teacher but also a guiding force who believed that education should reach every child, regardless of their background. Though he is no longer with us, his legacy continues to live on in the hearts of all who walk through the doors of Chhatrapati Shivaji Vidyamandir. His dream lives on — in our students, our teachers, and our mission.

Our Principal

We are proud to have Mr. Vijaykumar Jalindar Jagtap the esteemed Principal of Chhatrapati Shivaji Vidyamandir, Dhayati. A passionate educator and a respected academician, Mr. Jagtap brings with him years of experience, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to student success. Known for his exceptional command over Mathematics, he has a unique ability to make complex concepts clear and engaging. His teaching methods not only strengthen students' academic foundation but also ignite a love for problem-solving and logical thinking.
